Korth Group is a Canadian distributor representing some of the world’s leading brands in the shooting sports, hunting, and outdoor industries. Founded in 1977 and headquartered in Lethbridge, Alberta, the company connects top-tier manufacturers with retailers and consumers across the country. With a commitment to integrity, expertise, and long-term partnerships, Korth Group has built a trusted reputation as the link between global innovation and Canada’s outdoor community.
